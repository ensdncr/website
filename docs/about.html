About Me

Enes Dincer

Advertiser

Hi! I'm Enes Dincer, an advertiser based in Istanbul. I started work in an advertising agency at the age of 19. I worked as a photographer, online content manager, media buying planner, and graphic designer at some agencies. I'm a hardworker and self-motivated individual person who has a bachelor degree majoring in Radio, Cinema and Television from Faculty of Communication, Istanbul University. Right now I am seeking to gain some experiences in my career so I can exploit my ability in the sector that I love and dedicated in. In the same time I do hope I can use my education background in my career well. So I can't wait to work with all of you out there. Thank You.
